Welcome to the House of Black and White

Your training is about to begin.

Soon, you will receive a message with the name of your target and the means to their end: a word.

To give your target The Gift of the Many-Faced God, you must compel this word from their lips. Some means may be harder, but we only do this when we feel you are up to the task. Use your wits, not your might, we don't want to draw attention to whom is training to serve.

If you succeed, inform your target of their just demise, reveal your message to them as proof, and take a picture of their message for your own reference: their target is now your own.

If you fall, inform the Faceless of your demise.

You will please a man if you are the last alive. Good luck.

Check the Wall of Faces if you are not familiar with your target's name. And of course, be discreet.
